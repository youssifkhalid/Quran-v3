# Changelog — سكينة

## [3.0.0] — 2025
### Added
- **AI Islamic Assistant** — powered by Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 with Quran/Hadith citations
- **Quran verse search** — full-text search across 6,236 ayahs via alquran.cloud
- **Tafsir viewer** — 5 editions including Muyassar & Jalalayn, per-ayah expandable
- **Hadith Library** — 40 Nawawi hadiths with grade, narrator, keywords, favorites
- **Dua collection** — 20+ Quranic & Prophetic duas with categories, favorites, share
- **Daily Wird tracker** — custom goals, streak tracking, 14-day heatmap
- **Fasting tracker** — Ramadan grid (29/30 days) + voluntary fasting days
- **Islamic Calendar** — full Hijri calendar with 12 Islamic occasions
- **Islamic Names** — 80+ male/female names with meanings & origin
- **Quran Radio** — 8 live streams (Makkah, Madinah, Egypt, Afasy...)
- **Settings** — theme, font size, prayer method, notifications, data export
- **Responsive Layout** — desktop sidebar + tablet sidebar + mobile bottom nav
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — ⌘K search, ⌘Q quran, ⌘A AI, ⌘P prayer
- **Global audio player** — persistent MiniPlayer across all pages
- **Service Worker** — offline cache + prayer push notifications

### Improved
- Mushaf viewer: swipe navigation, keyboard arrows, jump to page
- Prayer page: daily tracker, weekly history, monthly schedule
- Quran index: list/grid toggle, khatmah integration, verse search card
- Adhkar: 11 categories, source attribution, share per-dhikr
- Tasbeeh: colored themes, session history, custom dhikr
- Qibla: live compass + distance to Mecca
- Reciters: featured bar, style filters, alphabetical sort
